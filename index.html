Interests and Expertise
My career goals are driven by my keen interest to have mastery of the science, methodologies, and principles of GIS and geoscience. Almost every real-world problem requires the what, where, and when questions to be reliably answered, and geoscience is not exempted. GIS is well suited to answer these questions and provides valuable information that is used to tackle real-world problems.

My geoscience (geophysics) background covers hydrological, environmental, engineering, mineralogical, and petroleum aspects of geophysics. I have had over five years of geoscience-related work experience.

GIS is my second major. Of course, the GIS far and wide spectrum of relevant skillsets has spurred me to remarkably advance my knowledge and applicability of remote sensing and programming with high-level languages like Python, R, HTML, and a few others.
